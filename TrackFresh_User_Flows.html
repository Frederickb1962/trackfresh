Receipt Scanner
Scan a grocery receipt to bulk-add food items with AI-estimated shelf life
1
Tap "📷 Scan Receipts" button
Located at the top of the Tracker tab — full-width button marked ⭐ Start here for best results.
2
Modal opens → Take Photo or Upload
Point camera at the paper receipt or upload an image from your photo library.
3
AI scans receipt Claude claude-sonnet-4-6
Extracts only food & beverage items. Skips non-food (toiletries, cleaning supplies, etc.). For each item determines: name, category, storage location, days sealed, days after opening, storage tips.
4
Review list of found items
Each item shown with: name, category pill, storage location, tip badges. Items are pre-selected.
5
Tap "Add to Tracker" — all items saved
All scanned items are added to Tracked Items with today as open date. Storage tips persist for each item.
💡 Best results: Lay receipt flat, good lighting, keep the full receipt in frame. Works with most supermarket / warehouse club receipts.
✅
Items appear in Tracker with expiry estimates and storage tip badges
📸
Smart Scan
Scan a single product — AI reads the barcode and/or label in one photo
1
Tap "📸 Smart Scan" button
Located in the 3-button row on the Tracker tab. Requests camera permission if not yet granted.
2
Choose scan mode
1️⃣ Single Scan — add one item, modal closes after.
📦 Multi Scan — camera resets after each add; keep scanning until done.
3
Point camera at product → Tap "📸 Take Photo"
Green guide box shows the ideal framing area. Works for barcodes, front-of-pack labels, or both visible at once.
4
AI analyzes photo /api/scan-smart
Claude detects barcode number if visible → Open Food Facts lookup for verified name/category.
Falls back to AI label reading if no barcode or OFO has no match.
Also reads expiration date, storage tips, days after opening.
5
Review result card
Shows: product name, source badge (📊 Barcode lookup / 🤖 AI label scan), category, storage tips as colored pills.
6
Set storage location
Fridge Freezer Pantry — pre-selected by AI, user can override.
7
Enter expiration date (if not auto-detected)
🎤 Speak Date — say e.g. "June 15 2026", fills automatically.
📅 Enter Date — opens native date picker.
📷 Scan Expiry Label — take a second photo of just the date stamp.
8
Tap "Add to Tracker" (single) or "Add & Next" (multi)
Single mode: modal closes. Multi mode: camera resets immediately for the next item. Tap "✅ Done (N items)" to finish.
✅
Item saved to Tracker with full details, tips, and expiry countdown
📦
Scan Multiple Items
One photo → AI identifies every product in frame at once
1
Tap "📦 Scan Multiple" button
Middle button in the 3-button row on Tracker tab.
2
Arrange products & take photo
Lay items on a counter, or line up on the shelf. Fit all products inside the wide green guide frame. Tap "📸 Take Photo".
3
AI identifies all products /api/scan-multi
Claude identifies every distinct food item it can see. For each item with a visible barcode: Open Food Facts lookup for enriched name & category. Returns array of all found items simultaneously.
4
Review scrollable list — "N items found"
Each item card shows:
  • Checkbox (checked by default — uncheck to skip)
  • Editable product name field
  • Source badge: 📊 Barcode or 🤖 AI scan
  • Category pill
  • Storage location dropdown (Fridge / Freezer / Pantry)
  • Use By date picker
  • Storage tip
5
Tap "✅ Add N items to Tracker"
Only checked items are added. Or tap "📸 Scan Again" to return to camera for another batch.
💡 Tip for barcodes: Face all barcodes toward camera. 3–6 items per photo is ideal — too many makes barcodes too small to read reliably.
✅
All selected items added to Tracker in one tap
✏️
Quick Add
Manually enter a food item without scanning
1
Tap "✏️ Quick Add" button
Right button in the 3-button row on Tracker tab. Opens inline form.
2
Type item name + optional voice 🎤
Text field for product name. 🎤 mic button dictates the name by voice.
3
Set quantity, category, location
Quantity text field · Category dropdown · Location dropdown (Fridge / Freezer / Pantry / Counter).
4
Enter expiration date + optional voice 🎤
Date picker field. 🎤 voice button listens for spoken date like "March 20 2026".
5
Tap "Add to Tracker"
Item saved. Form clears. Cancel button discards without saving.
✅
Item added to Tracker with manual details
🗂️
Tracker — Item Actions
What each button on a tracked item does
✏️ Edit
1
Opens Edit Item modal
Edit name, use-by date, quantity, location, category.
2
🎤 Speak Date button on date field
Say the expiration date aloud (e.g. "June 15 2026") — fills automatically. Button turns 🔴 while listening.
3
Save → updates item in Tracker
✅ Used It!
1
Removes item from Tracker
Item is marked as used and removed from the active tracked list.
2
Auto-adds to Shopping List
Item appears in "🔄 Recently Used — Restock" section of Shopping List with a 🔄 badge, reminding you to restock it.
🗑️ Delete
1
Removes item permanently
No restock reminder added to shopping. Item is deleted without confirmation.
🔍 Filter & Sort
1
Filter by location
Tap Fridge / Freezer / Pantry pills to show only items in that location.
2
Sort by expiry urgency
Items auto-sort: 🔴 Expiring ≤3 days · 🟡 Soon ≤7 days · ✅ Fresh.
⚠️ Expiry alerts: A red bell notification appears automatically when any item has ≤2 days remaining. Tap "Find Recipes" in the alert to go straight to recipe suggestions using expiring items.
🛒
Shopping List
Build your list manually, from expiring items, or from used items needing restock
Sections in Shopping List
🔥 Expiring Soon
Auto-populated by the Tracker. Shows items expiring within 7 days. Tap any item to add it to your main shopping list for repurchase.
🔄 Recently Used — Restock
Auto-populated when you tap "✅ Used It!" on a Tracker item. Shows items you recently finished so you remember to buy more.
Main Shopping List — Buttons
➕
Add Item button
Type item name in the input field at the top and tap ➕ to add to main list. Full-width input row.
✓
Check off item
Tap the checkbox to mark as bought. Item gets a strikethrough. Stays on list until cleared.
🗑️
Remove item
Tap trash icon to remove individual item from list.
🗑️
Clear checked
Removes all checked/bought items from the list in one tap.
💾
All shopping list data persists across sessions via localStorage

Recipe Suggestions
AI generates 5 recipes prioritizing your expiring items
Mode Selection
✅ Use What I Have
Strict mode. Only suggests recipes using tracked items already in your kitchen. Basic pantry staples (salt, oil, water) are allowed. No extra shopping needed.
🛒 Suggest Additional Ingredients
Flexible mode. Allows up to 1–2 extra ingredients to complete a dish. Extra items shown with real brand names (e.g. "need: Heinz Ketchup"). Your existing items marked ✓.
Flow
1
Navigate to Recipes tab
Tap 🍳 in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Select mode
Toggle between "Use What I Have" and "Suggest Additional Ingredients" using the mode buttons above the generate button.
3
Tap "🤖 Get AI Recipe Ideas"
Sends your tracked items list + selected mode to Claude AI. Items expiring soonest are prioritized.
4
5 recipe cards generated
Each card shows: name, description, ingredients (with ✓ for items you have), step-by-step instructions, estimated cook time, difficulty, and which expiring items it uses.
5
Save or use a recipe
Tap 💾 Save to bookmark the recipe. Tap "Add missing ingredients to shopping list" to send needed items directly to Shopping List.
📌 Saved recipes persist across sessions. Access them anytime from the Saved Recipes section at the top of the Recipes tab.
✅
Recipes tailored to what's expiring, with optional shopping list integration
📅
Meal Planner
Plan meals for the week using tracked items — AI can auto-suggest
1
Navigate to Meals tab
Tap 📅 in the bottom navigation bar.
2
Tap "🤖 AI Suggest Meals" (optional)
AI generates a week of meal suggestions based on your tracked items and any dietary restrictions set in Dietary Needs. Prioritizes items expiring soon.
3
Manually add meals per day slot
Each day has Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snack slots. Type or search a meal name and tap ➕ to add to that slot.
4
Tap "🛒 Add ingredients to Shopping List"
For any planned meal, tap this button to automatically send the meal's ingredients to your Shopping List — only adds items you don't already have tracked.
5
Remove / edit meals
Tap ✕ on any meal slot to remove it. Tap a meal name to edit it inline.
💡 Meal plan data persists across sessions. The plan is tied to the current week view.
✅
Week planned · Missing ingredients auto-added to Shopping List

Dietary Needs
Track dietary restrictions and preferences for every household member
Household Dietary Tags
Toggle on/off for the whole household:
🌾 Gluten Free 🥛 Dairy Free 🥦 Vegetarian 🌱 Vegan 🥜 Nut Allergy 🐟 No Seafood 🍖 Halal ✡️ Kosher 🌶️ Low Sodium 🩸 Diabetic Friendly 💪 High Protein 🍽️ Low Calorie
Family Member Profiles
1
Add family member
Type their name in the input field → tap ➕ Add. Member appears as an expandable card.
2
Expand member card → toggle their restrictions
Each member has individual dietary restriction tags (same set as household tags). Active restrictions shown as colored pills on the card summary.
3
Edit member name
Tap ✏️ on member card to rename inline. Tap ✓ Save or ✕ Cancel.
4
Remove member
Tap 🗑️ to remove the member and their restrictions from the profile.
🤖 AI integration: Dietary restrictions from both household settings and family member profiles are passed to the Meal Planner AI when generating meal suggestions, so recommendations respect all active restrictions automatically.
💾
All dietary data persists across sessions via localStorage
